Millennial Spending

2019-05-24

Sankey Diagrams

Sankey Diagrams are a popular way of showing flows. Lately they’ve become pretty popular for showing budgets since they allow you to show the hierarchical relationships and proportions within groups pretty intuitively. Below, I just wanted to provide a really quick example of one of these using data from the Consumer Expenditure Surveys. Speifically, the data used come from the 2017 Generational table to show how the average Millennial spends their money.

There are a couple of caveats:

  1. Alcohol was combined with food to make a higher-level food and drinks category
  2. Reading was moved into the entertainment category
  3. Several smaller categories were regrouped into a larger miscellaneous category

Other than that the data match exactly what’s in the official table. Enjoy!

The Sankey diagram was created using the very helpful R package networkD3
